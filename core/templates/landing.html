About SKUNOW

SKUNOW is an inventory intelligence platform built for startups, SMEs, and growing eCommerce brands. It centralizes purchasing, stock control, and channel reconciliation into a single system of record - eliminating spreadsheet chaos and fragmented tools.

Raise and manage purchase orders with full lifecycle tracking, monitor shipments from suppliers to warehouses or 3PLs, and receive stock with complete audit trails - all while maintaining accurate multi-location inventory balances.

Sync and reconcile inventory with Amazon and Shopify, detect stock drift early, and prevent costly stockouts or overselling before they impact operations.
